Reflection Assignment

How do you plan to build on your current traits and skills throughout your program?

I plan to continue to practice code with personal projects. I will also continue to study along side my peers in study groups as this is also excellent practice for me. Debugging and problem solving with my peers has been another effortless learning tool for me.
What do you aspire to be in the web developer field? Where do you aspire to work?

For starters, I aspire to work 100% remote. I recently began a small business and learning fullstack webdevelopment is crucial to my success. Not only will this enable me to design my OWN online platform but it will also enable me to travel and work for myself or anyone else, ANYWHERE!
What insights have you gained as a result of this course?

Prior to this class, I had no idea how much time, effort and problem solving technique went into web design. Since beginning this class, I almost always click into devoloper tools as I'm web surfing. This has been an extremely helpful tool for me to understand coding and web development
Which topic, activity, or assignment did you enjoy the most? What did you learn by creating or doing this activity?

Week 5 Responsive Web Designs were was definitely the most enjoyable assignments for me. Both taught me several ways to style my web pages and were alot of fun to practice with colors.

What was your biggest accomplishment in the course?

My biggest accomplishment in this course is making this fully responsive web page. I can finally get rid of these 3rd party apps I have been using to maintain an online platform for my business!
How does the information you've learned apply to your future goals?

The assignments and projects of this course have created a library of templates I can effectively utilize for future designs and projects. The importance of this information for my future goals is immeasurable.
